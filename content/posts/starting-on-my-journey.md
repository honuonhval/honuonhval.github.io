## Taking the first step {#taking-the-first-step}

I've decided not to wait and just push myself to start on OSCP. I know it'll be lot of work for the next 2 months but I'm excited.


## Inspirations {#inspirations}

First thing I'll share is someone that I'm really thankful he is giving back to the community.  [The Cyber Mentor (YouTube channel)](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC0ArlFuFYMpEewyRBzdLHiw) is the first person that gave a good foundational overview of the field.  I didn't know where to start nor even know what kind of things I could search for.  I always believed that one can figure things out with passion and perseverance but a good coach/mentor can expedite the process.  Of course, I do not know The Cyber Mentor and was not mentored personally, but his course (he first released it on Udemy but he has his own site, so I'll link to his site [here](https://academy.tcm-sec.com/p/practical-ethical-hacking-the-complete-course)) saved me so much time bringing ideas and tools together.  He gave me good trunk of tools and ideas that I am now able to reinforce and build upon with my own research and experiences (branches and leaves I suppose).

If anybody is feeling bit lost where to start with cybersecurity.  I highly recommend you check him out and thank him later.
